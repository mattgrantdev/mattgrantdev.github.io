My Playstation Trophy Showcase

Ever since I was a younger lad, the completionist in me was chasing that 100% in any game I played. Whether it was quests in Runescape, stars in Mario Galaxy, or characters in the Lego Star Wars games, I was gonna unlock and achieve it all.

These days, though I don't have as much time, I find I'm able to scratch this itch through the developer driven Trophy lists curated for the Playstation consoles.

That all being said, here's my sort of PSN Trophy Showcase. Is this really anything you need, or want, to know? Nah, not really, but it's here!

Also, much like other data on this site, you can find that it is updated straight from the PSN API automatically via a GitHub Worfklow I built.
